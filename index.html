The Five Elms

The Five Elms stands in the charming village of Weedon, Five miles from Aylesbury and on the cusp of The Chiltern Hills Here at the Five Elms we offer a quintessential Dining Experience, using local and sustainable produce with modern techniques and style. We are passionate about our aims which are to serve great food that has to be seasonal, inspirational and, above all, delicious.
